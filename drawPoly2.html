Draw lines separating fish body from fins as in the picture below

  • To draw the line use the mouse left button to click and connect points that separates the fins from the body.
  • To finish a line use the mouse left button.
  • You also have the opttions to undo a point, or clear all points.
  • There are 4 fins that may need to be seapareted from the body:

    • - Dorsal fin;

    - Pelvic fin;

    - Anal fin;

    - Caudal fin;

  • To help you identifie those fins they are labeled on the example below.

    • Your browser does not support the HTML5 canvas tag.
